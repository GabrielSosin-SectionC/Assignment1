Mushoku Tensei : Jobless Reincarnation

What started off for me as another generic isekai anime turned out to be a new favorite anime of mine. Containing tropes from generic isekais to even deeper stuff such as dealing with life, this anime is truly one I hope to see more of.

Vinland Saga

I think if anyone wants to watch an anime for the first time and they don't like the usual anime tropes, then they should definetly give Vinland Saga a chance. This anime is themed around the viking era and it truly deals with some philosophical issues.

The idea of Absolute Pacifism and that spilling blood only leads to both the victim and the killer's demise is portrayed in a philosophical nature through the character's struggles.
